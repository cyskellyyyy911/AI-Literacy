BERRY & CHERRY — Two Complementary Tracks

BERRY is an in‑house project focused on resume evaluation and candidate assessment. CHERRY is an external pilot of a video‑AI interviewer tool. Below we re‑categorize information for clarity.

Why Recruitment?

  • High‑volume, repetitive processes
  • Clear success metrics to measure impact
  • Rich data for AI training
  • Lean, growing SG recruitment team

Objectives

  • Improve recruitment efficiency
  • Ensure consistency in hiring standards

Project BERRY

Internal Completed

Developed internally for resume evaluation and candidate assessment. Built fast for early wins and integration.

Scope

  • Automated resume parsing and shortlisting
  • Structured candidate assessment experiments
  • ATS integration for workflow continuity

What Worked

  • 70–80% of AI‑shortlisted passed recruiter review
  • ~50% faster screening for high‑volume roles
  • Zero false negatives observed in trials

What Didn’t

  • Assessment scores lacked differentiation
  • Prompting precision needed improvement
  • In‑house diarization was unreliable

Key Takeaways

  • Build internally to move fast; integrate external strengths where needed
  • Hybrid approach works (e.g., third‑party diarization)
  • Prompt engineering is critical

Project CHERRY

External Pilot In Progress

Pilot of an AI interviewer tool (e.g., MyInterview) for video‑based competency assessments.

Why Pivot

  • Let AI handle early filtering; recruiters focus on sourcing & pipeline
  • Faster access to advanced features (diarization, soft‑skill scoring)

Strengths

  • Rankings broadly aligned with recruiter evaluations
  • Effective for hard‑skill heavy roles
  • Multi‑language support

Limitations

  • Weaker on soft‑skill / culture‑fit signals
  • Limited ability to read expressions / tone reliably
  • No single reliable cut‑off score

Conclusion & Next

  • Use AI as an early filter, not a final decision maker
  • Match tool to role type; one size does not fit all
  • Continue external pilots and hybrid integrations
